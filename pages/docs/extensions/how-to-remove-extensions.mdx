## How can I uninstall extensions?

Within `Extensions` you can see a list of installed extensions.
![](assets/images/extensions-list-entry.png)

You will see your installed extensions
![](assets/images/sittly-extensions-list.png)

Hit `Ctrl + O` (letter O) to open the context menu and select `Uninstall` to uninstall the extension. You can also visit the extension's github repo.
![](assets/images/sittly-extensions-list-context-menu.png)
